Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Ingrediants:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 2 teaspoon salt

  • Steps:
    1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
    2. Shift together the flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
    3. In a medium bowl, cream together the melted coconut oil, brown sugar, and white sugar until well blended.

  • Variations/Tips:
    1. Substitute dark chocolate chips for a richer taste.
    2. Add a pinch of cinnamon for a unique twist.
    3. Use gluten-free flour for a gluten-free version.
